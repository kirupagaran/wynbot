WynBot

Wyndham's Virtual Assistant

WynBot can be extended to do all functionalities of Wyndham website on top of a highly personalised used interaction such as;

  • Next bin collection date
  • Book hard waste collection
  • Can give you weather information
  • Information about Wyndham
  • Toilets
  • Community Centre
  • Smart Bins
  • Free Wifi locations
  • Child Care centres
  • Kindergartens
  • Libraries
  • Playgrounds
  • Dog off lead areas
